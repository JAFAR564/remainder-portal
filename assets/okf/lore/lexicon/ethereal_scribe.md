# Ethereal Scribe Lexicon

The official language of The Remainder Portal, *Ethereal Scribe*, merges Japanese phonetics, Greek cosmic weights, Romance grammatical structures, and Old English syntax. 

Below are the key foundational terms required for localized narrative processing:

### 1. Amatsukrion
- **Pronunciation**: Ah-mah-tsoo-kree-on
- **Meaning**: Divine celestial arbiter. Represents absolute order and systemic balance.
- **Gameplay**: Cosmic audit tool used to resolve localized reality and physical law conflicts.

### 2. Wyrd-Kaze
- **Pronunciation**: Weird-Kah-zeh
- **Meaning**: Destructive reality storm. Seen as a natural purging cycle of the universe.
- **Gameplay**: Sector-wide event that randomizes active physical parameters and climate tags.

### 3. Aetheromaru
- **Pronunciation**: Eye-theh-roh-mah-roo
- **Meaning**: Local reality-stabilizing anchor.
- **Gameplay**: Portable ritual item that establishes a localized safe zone of air, gravity, and ground.

### 4. Eld-Sombra
- **Pronunciation**: Eld-Som-brah
- **Meaning**: Residual kinetic imprint. Often associated with stealth guilds of the outer rings.
- **Gameplay**: Unlocks stealth modifiers and shadows active coordinate tracking in hostile areas.

### 5. Cyning-Bushi
- **Pronunciation**: Koo-ning-Boo-shee
- **Meaning**: Cybernetic sentinel / Mythic knight. Guardians of the old empire's gate structures.
- **Gameplay**: Sentinel class archetype that provides defensive bonuses and oath-shield capabilities.

### 6. Thalassovael
- **Pronunciation**: Tha-las-so-vale
- **Meaning**: Shimmering liquid-neon ocean. Highly unstable region.
- **Gameplay**: Imposes fluid drift physics and reduces travel speed.

### 7. Kami-Aether
- **Pronunciation**: Kah-mee-Eye-thehr
- **Meaning**: Cosmic consciousness source. The raw uncompiled flow of thoughts from the Void that gives rise to sentient entities.
- **Gameplay**: Used in high-tier crafting to infuse items with autonomous intelligence.

### 8. Void-Mane
- **Pronunciation**: Void-Mah-neh
- **Meaning**: Residual system heat/energy emitted from overloaded processing sectors.
- **Gameplay**: Environmental hazard that drains physical stamina or energy reserves.

### 9. Obsidia
- **Pronunciation**: Ob-sid-ee-ah
- **Meaning**: Hardened, carbonized stone blocks. The core structural building block of fortresses and keeps.
- **Gameplay**: High-strength building material used for constructing watchtowers and guild halls.

### 10. Komorebi-Code
- **Pronunciation**: Koh-moh-reh-bee-Code
- **Meaning**: Scattered light/data patterns filtered through collapsing sector partitions.
- **Gameplay**: Rare collectible anomaly that grants minor stat boosts or unlocks hidden dialogue paths.

### 11. Yugen-Sora
- **Pronunciation**: Yoo-gehn-Soh-rah
- **Meaning**: The unfathomable, dark digital sky filled with glowing network paths.
- **Gameplay**: Navigation aid; allows mapping long-range coordinates across sectors.
