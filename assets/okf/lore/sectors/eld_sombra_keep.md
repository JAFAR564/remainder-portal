# The Eld-Sombra Keep

A dark, imposing fortress of gothic stone and obsidian glass, defined by historical martial traditions and heavy dark fantasy themes. It is a sector of absolute physical law, iron-clad oaths, and high lethality.

## Geography and Landmarks
- **The Spire of Runic Fires**: Towering spires of black glass that exhaust the intense magical heat generated by the keep's underground vault chambers.
- **The Ash-Wastes**: A desolate, windswept gray desert surrounding the fortress, composed of the burnt dust of fallen kingdoms and shattered stone monuments from ancient siege wars.
- **The Star-Forge Courtyard**: A central courtyard where weapons and armor are refined. The furnaces burn with raw, subterranean void-fire, and the anvil is a massive block of a fallen, magnetic star.
- **The Great Hearth of Oaths**: A massive, glowing fire-pit in the keep's main hall. It is anchored by a colossal runic sword, serving as a sanctuary zone where no blades can be drawn.

## Sector Rules and Mechanics
- **Martial Lethality**: Combat in this sector follows strict physical realism. Injuries are permanent, and falling in battle inside the Keep carries high stakes—including the potential shattering of one's equipment and the loss of accumulated status.
- **Guild Treaties**: The keep is governed by player alliances who carve out territorial lines. Guilds write custom law codes into the stone walls, enforcing taxes, safe-havens, and trade rules.
- **The Ancestral Vaults**: Deep vaults sealed by runic locks that can only be opened by resolving historical trials, containing legendary swords and armor of past *Cyning-Bushi* sentinels.
