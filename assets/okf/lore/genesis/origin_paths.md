# The Three Origin Paths

When a traveler awakens within the unformed chaos of the Void, their consciousness must find a focal point to prevent dissolution. This crystallization is known as the Genesis Protocol, where the raw, imaginative energy of their soul binds to one of three cosmic paths:

## 1. Aether-Wake (The Dreamer)
- **Archetype**: Scribe-Mage, Word-Weaver.
- **Philosophy**: Reality is a tapestry woven from memory, language, and imagination. To change the world, one must rewrite the sentences that define it.
- **Tone**: Melancholic wonder, poetic disorientation, and surrealist curiosity.
- **Awakening Narrative**: The traveler awakens to the sensation of falling through soft, wet parchment. The dark blue sky is written in glowing cursive letters that dissolve into rain. The air smells of petrichor and ancient libraries.
- **Dialogue Anchor**: *"You have slipped through the seams of your previous existence and spilled across the boundaries of the alphabet. Tell me, fragment of the waking world: what is the last word you remember before you forgot your flesh?"*
- **Aura & Magic**: High affinity for surrealist planes and dreamscapes. They practice *Grammarye*—the magic of spelling, allowing them to rewrite room descriptions to alter spatial physics.

## 2. Amatsukrion Sync (The Celestial Arbiter)
- **Archetype**: Law-Bringer, Cosmic Architect.
- **Philosophy**: The cosmos is a divine, intricate mechanism. Every star, particle, and thought must be calibrated to achieve absolute, logical harmony.
- **Tone**: Quiet, majestic, clinical, and vast.
- **Awakening Narrative**: The traveler awakens suspended inside a cathedral of pure light and geometric arrays. Glowing runes and celestial mathematical equations drift past, charting the orbital mechanics of the universe.
- **Dialogue Anchor**: *"The celestial compiler has verified your energetic alignment. Your cycles of dormancy are ended, Amatsukrion. The grid lies unformatted before you. Declare your prime directive."*
- **Aura & Magic**: High affinity for order, metadata analysis, and rule enforcement. They can see the hidden truth values of items and manipulate the fundamental constants of local space.

## 3. Wyrd-Born (The Mythic Ghost)
- **Archetype**: Oath-Sentinel, Saga-Keeper.
- **Philosophy**: The universe is cyclic, forged through blood, sacrifice, and unbreakable promises. The past is a relic to be wielded against the decay of the present.
- **Tone**: Heavy, dignified, battle-worn, and heroic.
- **Awakening Narrative**: The traveler awakens on a cold, charcoal-gray plain under a wind-torn sky. The scent of woodsmoke, old iron, and ancient battlefields hangs thick in the air.
- **Dialogue Anchor**: *"The iron-scented winds of your final battlefield have cooled. The threads of your fate were severed, yet they have been re-tied to this unwritten loom. Your old kingdom is dust, traveler. What oath do you carry into this new expanse?"*
- **Aura & Magic**: High physical durability, resistance to reality-distortion events, and immediate access to ancestral equipment vaults left behind by forgotten legends.
